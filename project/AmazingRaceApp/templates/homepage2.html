About Amazing Race



A fast and interactive game where players race around the world to compete against each other to decrypt every clue and reach the target locations. The game tests each player's physical strength as they strive to be the first to arrive at each location as well as their mental strength to overcome the challenges that lie ahead.

We are a team of Software Engineers passionate about gaming and wanting to help bring games to life! We love the Amazing Race show and wanted to give everyone the opportunity to play. Check out the Amazing Race Youtube channel below for free Amazing Race content.
